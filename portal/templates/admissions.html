How to Apply

01

Submit Application

Take the first step toward joining the MySchool family. Submit your application today and let us guide your child’s journey toward academic excellence, personal growth, and lifelong learning

02

Send Documents

Submit the required documents securely to complete your application. Our team will review them promptly and guide you through the next steps

03

Interview Process

Our interview process helps us understand each student’s strengths, interests, and potential. It’s an opportunity for learners and parents to engage with our team and learn more about MySchool

04

Decision Letter

After reviewing your application and interview, we will send a decision letter detailing your admission status and next steps

Admission Requirements

  • Completed Application Form
  • Official School Transcripts from Previous school
  • SAT or ACT Scores (Optional for 2024-2025)
  • Two Letters of Recommendation
  • Personal Essay (500-650 words)
  • Application Fee ($20 non-refundable)
  • Interview (for selected programs only)

International students require additional documentation including TOEFL/IELTS scores and visa information.
