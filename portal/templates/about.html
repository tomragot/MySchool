Our Story

Educating Minds, Inspiring Hearts

Our story reflects a commitment to holistic CBC learning, shaping bright minds while nurturing strong values and compassionate hearts.

2022

A year of ideation and visualization on how to provide quality education rooted in values, excellence, and holistic development. Since its introduction, the school has continued to grow, inspire, and transform young learners into confident, capable future leaders.

2023

A foundational year of rebuilding, strengthening systems, and advancing our CBC mission.

2024

A year of growth, achievement, innovation, and strengthened community through CBC learning.

2025

A transformative year promising innovation, growth, and empowering CBC excellence for every learner.

Our Mission

To deliver holistic, competency-based education that nurtures knowledge, character, creativity, and lifelong learners.

Our Vision

To be a leading CBC school, shaping innovative, compassionate, and empowered lifelong learners.

Core Values

Academic Excellence

We strive for outstanding academic achievement by fostering curiosity, critical thinking, and a love for learning.

Community Engagement

We actively connect with and contribute to our community, fostering responsibility, collaboration, and positive impact.

Innovation

We embrace creativity and forward-thinking, encouraging new ideas and solutions to enhance learning experiences.

Global Perspective

We cultivate awareness, understanding, and respect for diverse cultures, preparing students as responsible global citizens.
