3:00 PM - 6:00 PM
Main Auditorium

Annual Science Exhibition

Our Annual Science Exhibition showcases the creativity, curiosity, and innovation of our students. Learners present experiments, projects, and discoveries, demonstrating scientific thinking, problem-solving skills, and a passion for exploration

MySchool builds meaningful partnerships with educational institutions, organizations, and global initiatives to enhance learning opportunities. Through collaborations, our students gain exposure to international perspectives, innovative programs, and shared expertise that enrich their educational experience.

Event Highlights

  • Interactive student presentations of scientific experiments
  • Special teacher by renowned Music Dr. Robert
  • Robotics competition with prizes for top three teams
  • Science demonstrations by faculty members
  • Exhibition of innovative student projects
